East Charlotte

Ambiguous but Distinctive

A somewhat ambiguous, but distinctive, area covering a large portion of the city's eastern end. East Charlotte contains the city's most diverse population and varying income levels. Much of the east side contains some of Charlotte's most interesting cultural development and virtually any kind of ethnic food can be found in this area.

Some popular areas in East Charlotte include:

Easthaven

Eastland

Hickory Grove

Idlewild

Matthews

Mint Hill

Ready Creek
